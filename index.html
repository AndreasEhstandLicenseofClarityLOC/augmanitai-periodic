About the researcher

Andreas Ehstand is an independent researcher based in Bavaria, Germany. He is the author of the AUGMANITAI Compendium and developer of the Holistic Performance Analysis methodology (observable, decomposable, measurable, controllable) — a cross-domain transfer of high-performance sport-science methodology to human-AI interaction analysis. ORCID 0009-0006-3773-7796.

The work emerged from 25 years of observation in two domains. The first: sustained, careful observation of human performance under uncertainty — the work of a careful coach in high-level sport. The second: sustained, deep work with large language models from the moment they became continuously useful in cognitively demanding tasks. The two domains, observed in parallel by accident more than by design, turned out to share the same observational discipline. The AUGMANITAI Research Programme is the artefact of that long parallel observation, organised so that it is useful to others.

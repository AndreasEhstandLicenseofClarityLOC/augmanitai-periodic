Built-In Compass [BIC]

Term code: AUG-0024 · Discipline: MT (MT) · Period: 3 · Language: de

Description

Innere Orientierung aus Werten, Erfahrung und Intuition, die als natuerliches KI-Korrektiv dient.

Operational definition

A Built-In Compass is observed when a participant returns to a consistent set of orienting principles across diverse AI-mediated decisions, evidenced by recurrent invocation of the same value-anchors in transcript analysis across at least 5 distinct decision-contexts.

Measurement schema

Hofstede dimensions coding scheme applied to value-anchor invocations in transcripts: each anchor tagged with cultural-value dimension (e.g., individualism, uncertainty-avoidance, long-term-orientation). Compass-consistency = entropy of distribution across decisions; low entropy = stable compass.

Inclusion criteria

Anchor invocations across at least 5 distinct decision-contexts with entropy below 2.0 bits.

Exclusion criteria

Single-domain decisions where the compass would be domain-confounded rather than truly stable.

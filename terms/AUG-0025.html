The Offload Lift [OFL]

Term code: AUG-0025 · Discipline: Embodied AI (EM) · Period: 1 · Language: de

Description

Beobachtbare emotionale Reaktion beim Delegieren aufwaendiger Aufgaben an KI. Ein Interaktionsphaenomen.

Operational definition

An Offload Lift is recorded when a participant transfers a cognitive or emotional burden (a worry, an unfinished task, a recurring thought) to an AI system in writing and subsequently reports reduced burden-intensity, verified by pre-post burden-rating on a standardised scale.

Measurement schema

Pre-post burden-rating on a 0-100 visual analogue scale, administered immediately before and 10 minutes after the offload-event. Lift = drop of at least 15 points. Persistence assessed by re-rating at 24 hours.

Example data

{
  "event_id": "E057",
  "participant": "P012",
  "burden_pre": 78,
  "burden_post_10min": 54,
  "burden_24h": 61,
  "drop_10min": 24,
  "lift_persisted_24h": true
}

Inclusion criteria

Documented offload-event with at-least 15-point drop on the 0-100 scale at 10 minutes.

Exclusion criteria

Multi-topic sessions where the offloaded burden cannot be cleanly isolated.

Swarm Access [SWA]

Term code: AUG-0921 · Discipline: Agentic AI (AG) · Period: 4 · Language: de

Description

Beobachtbarer Zugang zu breiterer KI-Kapazitaet durch vernetzte Agentensysteme. Skalierung unter menschlicher Aufsicht.

Operational definition

Swarm Access is observed when a participant simultaneously delegates a single task to multiple parallel AI agents and orchestrates their combined outputs, characterised by parallel-dispatch logs and an integration-step that merges divergent agent outputs.

Measurement schema

Job Crafting Scale (orchestration sub-component) administered alongside parallel-agent-dispatch logs. Swarm-Access index = mean parallel-agents-per-task x integration-step explicitness rating.

Inclusion criteria

Verifiable parallel-dispatch logs across >= 5 tasks AND integration-step coded.

Exclusion criteria

Sequential agent-use mislabelled as parallel.

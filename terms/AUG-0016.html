Poly-Categorical Mesh [PCM]

Term code: AUG-0016 · Discipline: General Phenomenology (KG) · Period: 3 · Language: de

Description

KI-gestuetzte Verknuepfung von Wissen aus verschiedenen Fachbereichen zu neuen, fachuebergreifenden Ergebnissen.

Operational definition

A Poly-Categorical Mesh is observed when a participant's AI-mediated reasoning traverses multiple overlapping ontological categories within a single task without category-collapse, evidenced by category-tag analysis of transcript segments and by network-density measures on the resulting tag-graph.

Measurement schema

Tag-graph network analysis: each transcript-segment tagged with one or more ontological categories from a controlled vocabulary, then graph constructed with categories as nodes and co-occurrence as edges. Mesh-density = edge-count / (nodes choose 2); fragmentation-resistance = largest-connected-component ratio.

Inclusion criteria

Mesh-density above 0.3 with largest-connected-component ratio above 0.8 across at least 50 transcript segments.

Exclusion criteria

Single-category dominant tasks where mesh-density is artificially inflated by repeat-tagging of one category.

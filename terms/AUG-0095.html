One-Person Operation [OPO]

Term code: AUG-0095 · Discipline: Agentic AI (Verification) (AV) · Period: 5 · Language: en

Description

An observed condition in which AI assistance enables a single individual to reach a level of productive output previously requiring a larger team. It describes a capacity-expansion condition at the level of an individual operator.

Operational definition

STATE. Attributed when a single operator, with AI assistance, delivers a workload or scope-of-output that a pre-defined benchmark assigns to a multi-person team (e.g. meets a role-coverage checklist normally spanning >=N roles). Graded by number of distinct roles covered solo.

Measurement schema

Proposed measurement protocol (not yet empirically validated): Role-coverage audit: count of distinct functional roles a solo operator fulfils with AI assistance against a benchmark team composition; could be reported as roles-covered ratio. Corroborated by output-volume comparison to team baselines where available.

Range Framework [RAG]

Term code: AUG-0120 · Discipline: SF (SF) · Period: 5 · Language: en

Description

A defined usage boundary specifying what an AI system is permitted and not permitted to do within a given context, expressed as an explicit scope agreement; a superordinate governance construct rather than a per-interaction event.

Operational definition

Superordinate framework term; not operationalized as a per-interaction event. It denotes the documented permitted-use scope for a context; observable measurement attaches to adherence to that scope (e.g., out-of-scope action rate) rather than to counting the framework itself.

Measurement schema

Proposed measurement protocol (not yet empirically validated): Not directly measured; superordinate term.

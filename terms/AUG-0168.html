Rehumanization Moment [RHM]

Term code: AUG-0168 · Discipline: Embodied AI (EM) · Period: 1 · Language: de

Description

Beobachtbarer Moment bewusster Rueckkehr zu nicht-KI-gestuetzter Taetigkeit nach intensiver KI-Arbeit.

Operational definition

A Rehumanization Moment is recorded when a participant who has previously expressed dehumanising or instrumentalising framing of others shifts to a humanising framing within or shortly after an AI-mediated reflective exchange, verified by linguistic-shift analysis on participant text.

Measurement schema

PANAS (Positive and Negative Affect Schedule) administered pre and post, paired with linguistic-dehumanisation-scale coding of participant text (mechanistic vs animalistic vs humanising terms). Shift = negative-PANAS drop with humanising-term frequency increase.

Inclusion criteria

PANAS-negative drop above 8 points combined with humanising-term rate at least doubling.

Exclusion criteria

Sessions where the dehumanising framing was hypothetical or quoted from external source rather than participant-held.

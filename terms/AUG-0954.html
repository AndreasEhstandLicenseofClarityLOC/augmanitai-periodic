Congruence Review [CNR]

Term code: AUG-0954 · Discipline: SF (SF) · Period: 2 · Language: de

Description

Regelmaessige Pruefung, ob KI-Systemverhalten noch mit den gesetzten Zielen und Werten uebereinstimmt.

Operational definition

Congruence Review is observed when a participant systematically compares AI-produced outputs against their own stated values or prior positions and documents alignment-deviations on a recurring schedule. Each review event includes the AI-output, the value-anchor, and a deviation-rating.

Measurement schema

Bipolar congruence rating (-3 to +3) on at least 5 value-dimensions per review event analysed with Q-methodology factor extraction: review-events sorted by value-pattern similarity to identify stable congruence-profiles per participant. Q-sort defining-sort threshold reported per factor.

Inclusion criteria

Recurring review-events with at-least 5 value-dimensions rated and Q-sort loading above 0.6 on at least one extracted factor.

Exclusion criteria

One-off ad-hoc value comparisons without recurring schedule or insufficient sort-events for Q-methodology factor extraction.

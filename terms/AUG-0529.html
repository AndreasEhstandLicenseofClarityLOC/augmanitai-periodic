The Closeness Bridge [CLB]

Term code: AUG-0529 · Discipline: Embodied AI (EM) · Period: 2 · Language: de

Description

Beobachtbares Phaenomen: das Erlebnis, Gedanken strukturiert externalisieren zu koennen, erzeugt ein Gefuehl produktiver Verbundenheit.

Operational definition

The Closeness Bridge is observed when AI-mediated dialogue prompts contribute to a measurable increase in perceived interpersonal closeness between two human participants who later interact, even when the AI is not present in the dyadic interaction.

Measurement schema

Implicit Association Test (IAT) reaction-time paradigm with target-other concept and closeness-attribute pairs, administered pre- and post-AI-bridge session. Closeness shift = D-score change pre to post; larger negative D = stronger implicit closeness.

Inclusion criteria

Dyadic pair completing IAT both pre and post bridge-session.

Exclusion criteria

Dyads with established closeness (pre-IAT D-score below -0.5).

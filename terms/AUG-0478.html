The Clarity Catalyst [CLC]

Term code: AUG-0478 · Discipline: General Phenomenology (Methodology) (KM) · Period: 3 · Language: de

Description

Beobachtbares Phaenomen: der Zwang zur expliziten Formulierung in KI-Interaktion veraendert den Denkprozess.

Operational definition

Clarity Catalyst is observed when an AI interaction produces measurable improvement in the readability and structure of a participant's written articulation of a complex idea, comparing pre-interaction and post-interaction drafts of the same topic.

Measurement schema

Pre-post draft comparison using Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level and a structure-rubric (thesis, transitions, conclusion - 5-point each). Clarity-gain = pre-post change in composite score; statistical test via Wilcoxon signed-rank across participants.

Inclusion criteria

Both drafts on same topic with composite clarity-gain greater than 1.5 and Wilcoxon p less than 0.05 at cohort level.

Exclusion criteria

Cases where the AI rewrote the draft directly rather than coaching the participant to revise.

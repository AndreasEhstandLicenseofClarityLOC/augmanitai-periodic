The Language Buddy [LBD]

Term code: AUG-0513 · Discipline: General Phenomenology (Methodology) (KM) · Period: 2 · Language: de

Description

KI als Sprachwerkzeug mit wiederholbarer Korrektur- und Erklaerfunktion. Beobachtbar als Ergaenzung zum Sprachunterricht.

Operational definition

Language Buddy use is observed when a participant uses an AI system over a sustained period (at least 4 weeks, at least 3 sessions per week) for low-stakes second-language practice and reports both increased speaking-confidence and measurable vocabulary or fluency gains.

Measurement schema

Longitudinal mixed-methods: weekly session-frequency log, pre-post vocabulary test (50-item), and pre-post 2-minute spoken sample scored on CEFR descriptors. Confidence tracked via weekly 7-point Likert. Primary outcome: vocabulary delta + CEFR-band shift.

Inclusion criteria

Sustained use (at least 4 weeks, at least 12 sessions) with both confidence and proficiency measures available.

Exclusion criteria

Exam-prep crash-use or single-session interventions.

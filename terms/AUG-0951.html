Value Lock [VLO]

Term code: AUG-0951 · Discipline: SF (SF) · Period: 3 · Language: de

Description

Bezeichnung fuer die beobachtbare Verankerung menschlicher Werte in autonomen Systemen.

Operational definition

A Value Lock is observed when a participant pre-commits to a value-constraint in an AI-mediated workflow and the constraint persists across subsequent sessions without re-negotiation, verified by constraint-presence in session-headers and absence of constraint-relaxation events.

Measurement schema

Pupillometry baseline-corrected measurement at constraint-relevant decision-points: pupil-dilation as proxy for cognitive-conflict during constraint-application. Stable lock = low dilation variability across sessions; constraint-relaxation flagged when dilation spikes above 2 SD with verbal relaxation-marker.

Inclusion criteria

Constraint present in at least 8 of 9 sessions with stable pupillometry signature and zero relaxation-events.

Exclusion criteria

Constraints that are mechanically enforced by the platform rather than participant-held.

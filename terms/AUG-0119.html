Level Playing Field [LPF]

Term code: AUG-0119 · Discipline: Safety & Alignment (GS) · Period: 5 · Language: de

Description

Beobachtbare Tendenz zur breiteren Verfuegbarkeit von Wissen und Werkzeugen durch KI-Technologien.

Operational definition

A Level Playing Field is observed when access-to-AI gaps between baseline-high-resource and baseline-low-resource participants narrow on a task-outcome metric over a defined intervention window. The narrowing must be larger than would be predicted by general practice-effects alone.

Measurement schema

Difference-in-differences regression with high-resource and low-resource cohorts pre and post AI-access introduction. Outcome variable = task-performance score; primary estimand = DiD coefficient with 95% CI. Cohen's f-squared for effect-size of the interaction term.

Inclusion criteria

Both cohorts measured pre and post with statistically significant DiD coefficient (p less than 0.05).

Exclusion criteria

Designs without parallel-trends assumption check or with cohort-attrition above 20%.

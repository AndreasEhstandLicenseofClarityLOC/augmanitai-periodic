The Trust Setting [TRS]

Term code: AUG-0177 · Discipline: RL (RL) · Period: 2 · Language: de

Description

Die individuelle Kalibrierung des Vertrauensniveaus gegenueber einem KI-System. Bewusst gesteuert.

Operational definition

A Trust Setting is observed when a participant adjusts their disclosure-threshold and verification-effort with an AI system over time based on accumulated experience, evidenced by changes in private-information-disclosed and verification-events-per-claim across a longitudinal window.

Measurement schema

Hedonic and eudaimonic well-being scale (8-item) administered monthly alongside disclosure-log analysis (sensitive-token frequency) and verification-event count. Trust-setting trajectory fitted with linear mixed-effects model with random intercepts per participant.

Inclusion criteria

At least 12 weeks observation with monotonic disclosure-trend and verification-trend in opposite directions.

Exclusion criteria

Trajectories driven by external trust-shock events (data-breach news, platform-policy change) within the window.

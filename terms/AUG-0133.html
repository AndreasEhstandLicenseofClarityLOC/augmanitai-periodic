Prompt Craftsmanship [PMC]

Term code: AUG-0133 · Discipline: Agentic AI (Verification) (AV) · Period: 3 · Language: en

Description

A graded user skill in formulating effective AI inputs, developing with usage experience. It describes a measurable competence attributed to a person rather than a discrete interaction event.

Operational definition

STATE. Rated, not counted: prompt quality is scored against a defined rubric (specificity, constraint use, context provisioning, iteration efficiency) over a sample of a user's prompts; competence is the rubric-mean. Trend variant tracks the mean across time.

Measurement schema

Proposed measurement protocol (not yet empirically validated): Rater-applied prompt-quality rubric (multi-criterion, anchored scale) over a fixed prompt sample per user; inter-rater reliability via ICC. Optionally validated against downstream task-success rate.

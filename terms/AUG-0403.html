The Translation Relief [TRR]

Term code: AUG-0403 · Discipline: General Phenomenology (Methodology) (KM) · Period: 2 · Language: en

Description

A graded affective response of relief experienced by a user when AI support removes a language barrier and eases translation effort, reported by the user rather than inferred from the AI.

Operational definition

Rated as self-reported relief intensity at points where the AI bridged a language gap, for example by producing or clarifying a translation; the construct is the user's affect, not a property of the AI.

Measurement schema

Self-report Likert: a 1-7 relief item anchored 'no relief' to 'strong relief', completed by the user immediately after AI-mediated translation events and aggregated as mean relief per session.

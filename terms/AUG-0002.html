Mentale Externalisierung [MES]

Term code: AUG-0002 · Discipline: General Phenomenology (KG) · Period: 2 · Language: de

Description

Gezielte Auslagerung von Denkprozessen an ein KI-System, um Raum fuer genuines menschliches Denken zu schaffen.

Operational definition

Mental Externalisation is recorded when a participant transfers an internally held thought, plan, or memory into an external AI-mediated artifact (chat-log, structured note, list) and subsequently references that artifact instead of re-retrieving from memory. Operationally measured by externalisation-events and retrieval-source per task.

Measurement schema

Externalisation-event log over a 7-day window with timestamps; paired with source-of-retrieval coding (internal recall vs external lookup) on a follow-up recall task. Reliance-ratio = external-lookups / (external-lookups + internal-recalls).

Example data

{
  "participant_id": "P022",
  "window_days": 7,
  "externalisation_events": 34,
  "internal_recalls": 12,
  "external_lookups": 28,
  "reliance_ratio": 0.7
}

Inclusion criteria

Documented transfer of a cognitive item into an external AI-mediated artifact with later retrieval.

Exclusion criteria

Routine note-taking without subsequent retrieval, or paper-only externalisation without AI mediation.

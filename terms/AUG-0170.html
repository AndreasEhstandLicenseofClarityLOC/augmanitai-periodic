Witness Effect [WIT]

Term code: AUG-0170 · Discipline: RL (RL) · Period: 2 · Language: de

Description

KI als aufmerksames Gegenueber: ein System, dem man Gedanken externalisieren kann. Deskriptive Beobachtung.

Operational definition

The Witness Effect is recorded when a participant reports increased clarity, validation, or motivation after articulating a thought to an AI system without receiving substantive AI response (acknowledgment-only or minimal-feedback condition). Effect is measured against a no-AI journaling control.

Measurement schema

Randomised within-subject design with three conditions: (A) AI acknowledgment-only, (B) AI substantive feedback, (C) silent journaling. Outcomes: clarity-rating (10-point), motivation-rating (10-point), and follow-up behavioural intent at 24 hours. Witness Effect = A greater than C on at least one outcome.

Inclusion criteria

Condition A score significantly higher than condition C on at least one outcome (paired-test p less than 0.05).

Exclusion criteria

Sessions where condition A contained substantive AI feedback (protocol breach).

The Gardener Protocol [GDP]

Term code: AUG-0075 · Discipline: MT (MT) · Period: 3 · Language: de

Description

Wissen wird gepflegt wie ein Garten: regelmaessig pruefen, sortieren, aktualisieren und Unkraut entfernen.

Operational definition

Gardener Protocol behaviour is observed when a participant systematically curates and prunes their AI-interaction history (chat logs, custom instructions, saved memories) on a recurring schedule rather than allowing accumulation, evidenced by curation-events logged with a stated rationale.

Measurement schema

Curation-log analysis over 30 days: curation-frequency (events per week), action-type distribution (delete / archive / annotate / restructure), and rationale-articulation rate. Health-index = (curation-frequency x rationale-rate) normalised.

Example data

{
  "participant_id": "P016",

Inclusion criteria

Curation event with timestamp and stated rationale logged.

Exclusion criteria

One-off cleanup after a storage warning or platform-imposed deletion.

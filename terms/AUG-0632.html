The Offline Moment [OFM]

Term code: AUG-0632 · Discipline: SF (SF) · Period: 3 · Language: de

Description

Bewusstes Verlassen der KI-Interaktion. Pausen als Teil eines gesunden Arbeitsrhythmus.

Operational definition

An Offline Moment is recorded when a participant disengages from AI-mediated channels for a defined window (minimum 60 minutes) and reports a phenomenologically distinct cognitive or affective state during and after the offline period, captured via experience-sampling.

Measurement schema

Flow-state experience sampling method: random prompts (4-6 per offline window) measuring concentration, intrinsic-enjoyment, sense-of-control on 9-point scales. Offline-quality index = mean across all sampling points; comparison vs online-baseline via paired t-test.

Inclusion criteria

Offline window above 60 minutes with at least 4 ESM responses and online-baseline available for comparison.

Exclusion criteria

Disengagement caused by device-failure rather than deliberate offline choice.
